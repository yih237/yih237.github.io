Red (Taylor's Version) is the second re-recorded album by American singer-songwriter Taylor Swift, released on November 12, 2021, through Republic Records. It is a re-recording of Swift's fourth studio album, Red, following her first re-recorded album, Fearless (Taylor's Version), which was released in April 2021.
