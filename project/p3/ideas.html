What is the context / story?

Street photography is taking the everyday and the mundane and making it into something unique and beautiful. With something around every corner, New York City is the international hotspot of the world for street photography. The city is sometimes gritty, sometimes beautiful, and consists of such a diverse population, as well as architectures and neighborhoods in vastly different styles.

What is the content?

The content of this collection is based around New York City, the most photographed city in the whole world. This collection mainly focuses on the past appearance of the city as well as features different artists' approach towards street photography in NYC.
The different artists all have different takes on captureing the city, and they would focus on different subjects, therefore the many different views of the same city is presented.

What is the quality of image? How are they created?

The images are candid images shot around the city with a 135mm camera (one sub collection is in medium format, not sure to keep or not), mostly in color film.
